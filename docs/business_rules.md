### Business rules :

1. Customers must have a valid driver's license to rent a car.
2. Customers must be at least 21 years old.
3. Customers must provide a phone number and national ID.
4. Customers cannot rent more than 2 cars at the same time.
5. Each car must have a unique plate number.
6. Car pricing depends on category (Economy, SUV, Luxury, etc.).
7. Cars cannot be rented if their status is "Under Maintenance."
8. Cars must undergo maintenance every 10,000 km.
9. The system must record the car's mileage before and after each rental.
10. Cars must be checked for fuel level before and after rental.
11. Rental contracts must include a start date and end date.
12. A rental contract cannot end before its start date.
13. Rentals cannot exceed 30 days unless approved by an admin.
14. Reservations expire if the customer does not pick up the car within 24 hours.
15. Late returns incur a 20% surcharge per day.
16. Damage fees apply if the car is returned with issues.
17. Monthly rentals receive a 10% discount.
18. Employees must log in to create a rental contract.
19. Only admins can add or delete cars from the system.
